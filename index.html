Flourish

Your family's daily ritual — Boaz & Noah
We say and do things to allow our family to flourish.

We make Jesus important.

We love each other. We are kind to each other. We help each other. We care for each other.

We are intentional about...
  • Building trusting relationships through intentional bonding time together as a family and with each other.
  • Nurturing each others potential through quality time and learning.
  • Focusing on what improvements we need to make in ourselves and model the right behavior to each other.
  • Having fun and laughing together.
We remember to...
  • See the good and treasure differences.
  • Pause, and respond based on principles and desired results.
  • Stay true, in the moment of choice, to your commitments.
  • Share stories.
  • Tell me more about that.
  • Goal: Go with them and ask later.
